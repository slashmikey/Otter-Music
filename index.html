Hreeshyat Talukdar

Stage Identity: Otter

Hreeshyat Talukdar (Otter) is a 19-year-old independent artist from Assam, India, exploring the edge where emotion meets technology. Blending modern pop textures with cinematic atmospheres and catchy hooks, he creates fresh releases that feel both futuristic and personal.

Working with AI-powered tools like Suno AI and ElevenLabs as part of his creative process, Hreeshyat shapes ideas into full tracks—then refines them with an artist’s ear for mood, pacing, and replay value.

With each new release, he’s building a sound that’s curious, bold, and unmistakably his own.
